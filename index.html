Types of Coffee

There are four main types of coffee beans: Arabica, Robusta, Liberica, and Excelsa. Arabica is known for its smooth flavor and aroma, while Robusta is bolder and has a higher caffeine content. Liberica and Excelsa are less common but offer unique flavors.

Bean Varieties

Coffee production begins when the seeds of the Coffea plant's fruits (coffee cherries) are separated to produce unroasted green coffee beans. The beans are roasted and then ground into fineparticles.Additionally, the method of processing used may have an impact on the flavor of the coffee beans.

Coffee & Pastry

Everywhere in the world, people love the traditional pairing of coffee and pastry. Coffee's strong or delicate undertones are complemented by the rich tastes and textures of pastries, making for a pleasant meal. You should enjoy the process of find the ideal combination of coffee and pastries.
